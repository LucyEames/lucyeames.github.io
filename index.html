About me

Hi there, I'm Lucy, a Level 4 Computer Games Programming student at Staffordshire University. I'm proficient in coding in several languages, including C++ and Ruby. I became interested in video games when I played Pokemon LeafGreen as a kid and have been hooked ever since. Most of the games I play are of the RPG or platformer genres, with story-focus and puzzles involved. This includes Pokemon, as mentioned previously, as well as Fire Emblem, Ace Attorney and Hollow Knight, but there are always new games to discover.
